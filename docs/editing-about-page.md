# Editing the About Page in Contentful

This guide explains how to update the About Us page content without any coding knowledge.

## Accessing Contentful

1. Go to [app.contentful.com](https://app.contentful.com)
2. Log in with your credentials
3. Select the **Mama Tulia** space
4. Click **Content** in the left sidebar
5. Find and click on the **About Page** entry

## Editable Fields

### Hero Section (Top of Page)
| Field | What it does | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Page Title** | The main heading at the top | "About Mama Tulia" |
| **Hero Subtitle** | The text below the title | "Walking alongside mothers of premature babies..." |
| **Hero Image** | Background image (optional) | Upload a photo |

### Statistics Bar
These numbers appear in a row below the hero. Leave blank to hide.

| Field | What it does | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Stat 1 - Value** | First number | "500+" |
| **Stat 1 - Label** | First label | "Mothers Supported" |
| **Stat 2 - Value** | Second number | "1000+" |
| **Stat 2 - Label** | Second label | "Hospital Visits" |
| **Stat 3 - Value** | Third number | "50+" |
| **Stat 3 - Label** | Third label | "Home Visits Monthly" |
| **Stat 4 - Value** | Fourth number | "8+" |
| **Stat 4 - Label** | Fourth label | "Years of Service" |

### Mission Section
| Field | What it does | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Mission Section Title** | Heading for mission | "Transforming Lives Through Love" |
| **Mission Text** | Your mission description | Write 2-3 paragraphs. Separate paragraphs with a blank line. |
| **Mission Section Image** | Photo next to mission text | Upload a photo |

### Vision Section
| Field | What it does | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Vision Section Title** | Heading for vision | "A Future of Hope" |
| **Vision Text** | Your vision statement | 1-2 sentences about your vision |

### Values Section
| Field | What it does | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Values Section Title** | Heading for values | "Our Values" |
| **Values Text** | Your core values | List your values in a sentence |

### Call to Action (Bottom Banner)
| Field | What it does | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Call to Action Title** | Banner heading | "Join Our Mission" |
| **Call to Action Description** | Banner text | "Partner with us to support more preemie mothers..." |

## How to Edit

1. Click on any field to edit it
2. Type your new content
3. Click **Publish** (green button, top right) to make changes live

## Tips

- **Paragraphs**: In text fields, press Enter twice to create a new paragraph
- **Images**: Click the image field, then "Add existing" or "Add new" to upload
- **Preview**: Changes appear on the website within 1 minute after publishing
- **Undo**: If you make a mistake, click the three dots menu and select "Unpublish" or use version history

## Need Help?

Contact your website administrator if you have questions about editing content.
